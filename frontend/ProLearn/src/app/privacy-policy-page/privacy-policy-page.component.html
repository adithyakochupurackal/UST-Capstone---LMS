Privacy Policy

At ProLearn, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you visit our website, create an account, make a purchase, or use our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and other identifying details.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and account management details.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details and billing address.
  • Usage and Location Data: Information about website interactions and location (if enabled).
  • Communication Data: Support requests and feedback.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Improve services: Personalize user experience and optimize website performance.
  • Communicate: Send updates, offers, and provide customer support.
  • Process transactions: Manage accounts and verify identity as necessary.
  • Comply with legal obligations: Adhere to applicable laws and regulations.

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure storage. While we strive for the highest security standards, no method is 100% secure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties assisting in our operations.
  • Legal Compliance: As required by law.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers or acquisitions.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve site performance and personalize your experience. You can control cookie usage through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request your personal data and how we use it.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

7. Retention of Data

We retain your data only as long as necessary for business purposes or legal requirements, after which it is securely deleted.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy, and changes will be posted on this page. Please review it periodically.

10. Contact Us
